**FPL Companion App - Feature Scope Document**

*The purpose of this document is to scope and organize a broad set of features for an FPL (Fantasy Premier League) companion app focused on fun, usability, and smarter squad management without engaging in predictive modeling or regression analytics.*

---

## Core Tenets

* **No predictive models**: The tool will not attempt to forecast outcomes or use statistical modeling to rate teams or players.
* **Focus on usability and fun**: Emphasis on experience, utility, and engagement rather than precision forecasting.
* **Enrich FPL engagement**: Help users manage squads, plan strategically, and interact meaningfully with their league and rivals.

---

## Feature Themes & App Sections

### 1. **Squad Management & Planning**

*Enhance the userâ€™s ability to understand, visualize, and plan their team over time.*

**Key Features:**

* **Multi-Week Planner**: Interactive view to simulate multiple future gameweeks, show team structure over time.
* **Chip Optimization Assistant**: Suggest optimal chip usage windows (Wildcard, Bench Boost, etc.) based on fixture congestion or doubles/blanks.
* **Injury & Rotation Tracker**: Visual and alert system for flagged/injured players and rotation-prone players.
* **Team Consistency & Synergy**:

  * Highlight link-up players (e.g. provider + scorer combinations).
  * Tag players with traits like "Bonus Point Hunter", "Set-Piece Taker", etc.
* **Return Breakdown**: Graphical stats on how each player earns points (goals, assists, clean sheets, BPs).
* **Enhanced Stat Visualizations**: Trends for minutes played, points per 90, price changes, etc.

### 2. **Transfers & Squad Value Economy**

*Insight-driven tools to help users optimize team value and transfer moves.*

**Key Features:**

* **Transfer Reminder & Alerts**: Notifies users about transfer deadlines, unused transfers, chips, and team issues.
* **Player Comparison View**: Simple UI to compare player stats, fixtures, recent form.
* **FPL Economy Module**:

  * Track team value over time, gains from early transfers.
  * Correlate player popularity (transfers in/out) with price changes.
  * Visual market graphs: price volatility and trending players.

### 3. **Mini-League & Cup Insights**

*Encourage rivalry and league-based engagement through insightful summaries and comparisons.*

**Key Features:**

* **Dynamic League Standings**: Live update of league rankings (including autosubs and bonus predictions).
* **League Transfer Round-Ups**:

  * Who took hits, who made successful swaps.
  * Fun summaries like "Biggest Blunder" or "Top Transfer Mastermind".
* **Cup Matchup Analysis**:

  * Weekly preview of Cup opponent's team.
  * Visual comparison and threat levels.
* **Live Comparative Views**:

  * During GWs, compare your points vs rivals, who still has players left, captain differences, etc.
* **Ownership & Differential Analysis**:

  * Highlight differentials at global and mini-league level.
  * Trend analysis over recent weeks.
* **Targeted Milestone Tracker**:

  * "You need X pts this week to enter Top 100K"
  * Distance from league leader, target OR milestones.

### 4. **Live Experience & Real-Time Stats**

*Keep users engaged during matchdays and enhance the live FPL experience.*

**Key Features:**

* **Live Score Feed**:

  * Player-by-player score updates during GWs.
  * Bonus point predictions, captaincy points in real-time.
* **Match Event Alerts**:

  * Push/in-app notifications for goals, assists, cards, clean sheet losses.
* **Fixture Difficulty Ticker**:

  * Visual calendar of upcoming opponent difficulty.
  * Allow filtering by position, team, and custom difficulty settings.

### 5. **Social & Editorial Layer**

**Key Features:**

* **Achievements & Badges**:

  * Season history, chip usage timeline, most used formations, favorite players.
